Our Story

We are a couple of hookah addicts. our experience in smoking hookah is extensive to say the least. While hanging out one day, we started (as a joke) a simple challenge to see if we can making tobacco.

After a year of research and development, we started coming up with a decent quality tobacco.

Now comes the HARD PART!

I want to say that our biggest critics so far, and still are, our friends and family . after getting these guys on board to love the product (which took about a year or so). we decided to open a small factory in Phoenix Arizona.

With no clue about the process of obtaining a license to manufacture tobacco ! This nightmare of getting a federal license took two and a half years of pain and suffering.

Now here we are a full manufacture of hookah tobacco. From tobacco leafs to a mouth full of flavors.
